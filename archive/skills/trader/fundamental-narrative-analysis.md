# Fundamental Narrative Analysis

## Purpose

Turn raw business, thematic, macro, and catalyst information into a usable narrative that explains why a stock should matter now.

## Focus

- what the story is
- why the market might care now
- what could extend or break the story
- how narrative aligns or conflicts with tape and players
- what belongs in Airtable `Insights`

## Tool Resolution

Primary tools:
- `~/workspace/tools/trader/narrative.py`
- `~/workspace/tools/trader/macro.py`
- `~/workspace/tools/trader/api.py`
- `~/workspace/tools/trader/journal.py`
- `~/workspace/tools/trader/airtable_client.py`

## Rule

Narrative should explain the move, not excuse it. If the story and the tape disagree, say so.
