# Research Brief

## Use When

- Boss O asks for research on a topic
- a decision needs background before action

## Load First

- `skills/personal-assistant/CLAUDE.md`
- `~/.claude/skills/personal-assistant/research.md`
- `~/.claude/skills/personal-assistant/insight-synthesis.md`

## Workflow

1. Clarify the decision or output the research should support.
2. Gather the minimum relevant sources.
3. Separate facts, assumptions, and open questions.
4. Compress the result into a brief Boss O can use quickly.

## Deliverable

- key findings
- supporting facts
- open questions
- suggested next step
