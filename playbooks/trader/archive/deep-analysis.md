## Workflow

1. Restate why the ticker is on the table.
2. Read structure, broker behavior, orderbook, and urgency.
3. Build the thesis in plain language.
4. Define entry zone, invalidation, target, and position-sizing logic.
5. Decide whether the verdict is `buy`, `sell`, `hold`, `watch`, or `no edge`.

## Deliverable

For each ticker, produce:

- thesis
- key signals
- verdict
- clear risk
- next condition to watch

## Safety

- No autonomous execution.
- If the setup is weak, say `no edge`.
