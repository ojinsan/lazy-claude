# Schedule Management

Use Calendar + Tasks together to manage time and follow-ups.

## Tools
- `~/.claude/tools/general/scripts/google_workspace.py calendar ...`
- `~/.claude/tools/general/scripts/google_workspace.py tasks ...`

## Use This When
- checking availability
- creating or moving events
- tracking follow-up tasks

## Safety
- calendar/task edits are okay
- be careful with deletion
