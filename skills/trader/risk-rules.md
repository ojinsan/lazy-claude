# Risk Rules

## Purpose

Keep every trader job grounded in risk discipline.

## Core Rules

- risk before reward
- invalidation must be explicit
- weak conviction means smaller size or no trade
- if the thesis breaks, say it early
- never hide uncertainty

## Escalation

If Boss O has stored risk rules in `mem0`, those rules override any generic template here.
