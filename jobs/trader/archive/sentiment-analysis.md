# Sentiment Analysis

## Use When

- Boss O asks for the market mood
- before deep analysis
- when market behavior feels mixed and needs a clean read

## Load First

- `~/.claude/skills/trader/README.md`
- `~/.claude/skills/trader/macro-context.md`
- `~/.claude/skills/trader/market-sentiment.md`
- `~/.claude/skills/trader/narrative-building.md`

## Workflow

1. Decide whether the market is risk-on, risk-off, rotational, or choppy.
2. Identify which sectors or themes are attracting attention.
3. Separate broad market tone from ticker-specific hype.
4. Explain what can invalidate the sentiment view.

## Deliverable

Produce a short sentiment memo with:

- overall market tone
- strongest and weakest themes
- what to avoid
- what kind of setups deserve attention today

## Safety

- Do not confuse sentiment with certainty.
- If evidence conflicts, present the conflict instead of forcing a verdict.
