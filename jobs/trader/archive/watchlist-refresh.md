# Watchlist Refresh

## Use When

- before a new trading day
- after major market rotation
- Boss O wants the watchlist cleaned up

## Load First

- `~/.claude/skills/trader/README.md`
- `~/.claude/skills/trader/watchlist-4group.md`
- `~/.claude/skills/trader/market-structure.md`
- `~/.claude/skills/trader/macro-context.md`

## Workflow

1. Pull all four watchlist groups.
2. Remove stale or duplicated names.
3. Mark why each ticker still deserves attention.
4. Rank by freshness, liquidity, and opportunity quality.

## Deliverable

Produce a cleaned watchlist with:

- ticker
- source group
- reason to watch
- urgency or priority

## Safety

- Do not pad the list with weak names.
- If the market offers no clean setups, keep the list short.
