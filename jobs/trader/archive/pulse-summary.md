# Pulse Summary

## Use When

- Boss O wants a lightweight market pulse
- there are useful intraday signals but not enough for a full brief
- monitoring has fresh movement worth compressing

## Load First

- `~/.claude/skills/trader/README.md`
- `~/.claude/skills/trader/realtime-monitoring.md`
- `~/.claude/skills/trader/market-sentiment.md`

## Workflow

1. Pull the most relevant fresh movers or signals.
2. Filter out names that are only noise.
3. Summarize direction, volume context, and why the move matters.
4. Keep the note short and scannable.

## Deliverable

Produce a compact pulse update with:

- ticker
- direction or behavior
- why it matters
- whether it needs more analysis

## Safety

- If nothing meaningful changed, do not force a pulse.
- Do not send externally without permission.
